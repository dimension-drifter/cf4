# Kriti AI - Intelligent Hotel Reservation and Management System

## Overview

Kriti AI is an enterprise-grade artificial intelligence voice agent platform designed specifically for the hospitality industry. The system leverages advanced natural language processing, real-time speech recognition, and machine learning algorithms to automate hotel and restaurant reservations through natural voice conversations. This solution enables hospitality businesses to provide 24/7 booking services, deliver personalized guest experiences, and optimize revenue through intelligent pricing negotiations.

## Key Features

### Intelligent Voice Reservation System

- **Natural Language Understanding**: Advanced NLU engine capable of understanding complex booking requests, including multi-room reservations, special accommodations, and custom requirements
- **Multi-Turn Conversations**: Maintains context throughout extended dialogues, allowing guests to modify requests, ask questions, and refine their bookings naturally
- **Real-Time Availability Checking**: Instant synchronization with property management systems to provide accurate room and table availability
- **Multi-Language Support**: Native support for over 40 languages with automatic language detection and seamless switching

### Dynamic Price Negotiation Engine

- **Intelligent Pricing Algorithms**: AI-driven negotiation capabilities that balance guest satisfaction with revenue optimization
- **Configurable Negotiation Parameters**: Define minimum acceptable rates, discount thresholds, and negotiation boundaries per room type, season, or guest tier
- **Competitive Rate Matching**: Automatic comparison with market rates to inform negotiation strategies
- **Approval Workflow Integration**: Escalation protocols for negotiations exceeding predefined thresholds

### Personalized Guest Experience

- **Guest History Analysis**: Comprehensive analysis of previous stays, preferences, and spending patterns
- **Predictive Offer Generation**: Machine learning models that predict guest preferences and generate tailored promotional offers
- **Loyalty Program Integration**: Automatic recognition of loyalty status with corresponding benefits and exclusive offers
- **Special Occasion Recognition**: Detection and acknowledgment of birthdays, anniversaries, and other significant dates

### Automated Confirmation System

- **SMS Confirmation Delivery**: Instant text message confirmations containing reservation details, confirmation numbers, and relevant property information
- **Email Confirmation Generation**: Professionally formatted email confirmations with calendar integration support
- **Multi-Channel Notifications**: Support for WhatsApp, messaging apps, and other communication platforms
- **Confirmation Customization**: Branded templates with dynamic content insertion
